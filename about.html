About STACKLY

Built by analysts who got tired of noisy dashboards.

STACKLY started in 2019 when three SOC analysts left their enterprise jobs frustrated with tooling that generated alerts nobody had time to read. We built the platform we wished we'd had — one that filters noise, verifies threats with real humans, and tells you exactly what happened and what to do next.

Our Approach

Signal over noise.

Automated tools flag everything; only a fraction matters. STACKLY combines detection engineering with human triage so every alert that reaches you is confirmed, contextualized, and actionable.

  • Every critical alert reviewed by a human analyst before escalation
  • Clear, jargon-free incident summaries — not raw log dumps
  • Transparent SLAs for detection and response time
